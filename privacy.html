Payment Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Payment Tracker respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data outside your devices. All financial data, reminders, and transactions you enter are securely stored on your device and optionally synced to your private iCloud account through CloudKit.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. If you enable iCloud, your data is synchronized across your devices via Apple’s secure CloudKit infrastructure. No one, including the app developer, can access your data.

Analytics and Tracking

Payment Tracker does not use analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking technologies.

Permissions

The app may request access to your Calendar to display payment reminders or related events. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded or shared.
