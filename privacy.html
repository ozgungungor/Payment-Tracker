Payment Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Payment Tracker values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled within the app, including information related to third-party services such as Google AdMob.

Data Collection and Usage

Payment Tracker itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data outside your devices. All financial data, reminders, and transactions you enter are stored securely on your device and optionally synchronized with your private iCloud account through CloudKit.

Advertising (AdMob)

Payment Tracker uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data, such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and approximate location, to provide and personalize ads. This data collection and processing are governed by Google’s privacy policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can also review Google’s AdMob privacy policy here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

Your personal financial data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. If you enable iCloud synchronization, your data is transferred securely through Apple’s CloudKit service. Neither the developer nor any third party has access to this data.

Permissions

The app may request access to your Calendar to create or display payment reminders. This permission is optional and data remains on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

Aside from AdMob’s ad-serving mechanisms, Payment Tracker does not use any analytics tools or tracking frameworks.
